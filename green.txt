She had bright green eyes that flickered on faintly even
 in complete darkness, and Alex was never quite sure
 whether it was an issue of image persistence on her LCD
 screens, or if perhaps that viscous green mass they
 knew inhabited her skull might be shining through.
 Or possibly tiny elves conducting experiments with
 electricity in the quite chambers of her brain. The
 organisation largely financed itself with these
 sorts of rental arrangements.
