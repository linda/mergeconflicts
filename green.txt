She had bright green eyes that glowed faintly even
 in complete darkness, and Alex was not sure
 whether it was because of image persistence on her LCD
 screens, or if perhaps that viscous green mass they
 suspected inhabited her skull might be shining through.

 Or possibly small gnomes conducting experiments with
 magic in the quiet chambers of her brain. The
 organisation largely financed itself with these
 sorts of rental arrangements.
